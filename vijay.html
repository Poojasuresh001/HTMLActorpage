Vijay, popularly known as Thalapathy Vijay, is one of the most celebrated actors in the Tamil film industry. Born on June 22, 1974, as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, he is the son of veteran filmmaker S.A. Chandrasekhar. Vijay made his acting debut as a child artist and later emerged as a leading actor with his breakthrough film Poove Unakkaga (1996).
